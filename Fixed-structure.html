My Favorite Places in the Bay Area

Map 1:

Sibley Regional Volcanic Preserve hosts some incredibly unique landscapes and a complex network of hiking trails with views of the Golden Gate Bridge.

Map 2:

Limantour Beach is a remote beach in Point Reyes National Seashore. The trail to this beach is one of my favorite parts, as it takes you through a beautiful salt marsh that supports diverse wildlife.

Map 3:

Stinson Beach is one of the most popular beaches in the Bay Area. Its location near Mt. Tam in Marin County means it is accessible, yet still relatively untouched.
