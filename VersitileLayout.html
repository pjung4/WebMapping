There are many fun things to do around Washington DC!

This map will direct you to several locations around the capital that may be of interest if you are looking for something to do.

Here you can see where you might find a good bar, theatre, or music venue. One of the city's best bicycle routes is also displayed. These routes can be helpful to get around, but may also just present you with an opportunity to get outside and see the city!
